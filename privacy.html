Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how HK Jewelry Corp ("HK Jewelry Corp," "we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, stores, and protects personal information when you use hkjewelrycorp.com (the "Site"), contact us, or conduct business with us.

1. Information We Collect

  • Contact Information: name, email address, phone number, and mailing/shipping address you provide.
  • Inquiry and Transaction Information: product interests, quotes, order details, and service history.
  • Technical Information: IP address, browser/device data, approximate location, and website interaction data.
  • Communications: messages and correspondence sent through forms, email, or phone.

2. How We Use Information

  • Respond to inquiries and provide customer support.
  • Prepare quotes, process transactions, coordinate delivery or pickup, and provide after-sales service.
  • Improve our Site performance, usability, and security.
  • Send service communications and, where permitted, promotional messages.
  • Comply with legal obligations and prevent fraud or abuse.

3. Legal Bases and Consent

Where required by applicable law, we process personal information based on one or more lawful bases, including your consent, performance of a contract, compliance with legal obligations, and our legitimate business interests.

4. Cookies and Analytics

We use cookies and similar technologies to operate the Site, understand traffic, and improve user experience. You can control cookies through your browser settings; disabling cookies may impact certain Site functionality.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell personal information to third parties. We may share personal information only as necessary with service providers (for example, hosting, payment processing, shipping, communications, and analytics), legal authorities when required by law, or as part of a business transfer permitted by law.

6. Payment Information

Payment card data is processed by authorized payment processors and is not stored in full on our servers. Transactions may be subject to verification and fraud-prevention checks.

7. Data Security

We apply administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect personal data against unauthorized access, loss, alteration, or disclosure. No method of electronic transmission or storage is completely secure.

8. Data Retention

We retain personal information for as long as reasonably necessary to provide services, maintain business records, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and satisfy legal, tax, and accounting obligations.

9. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to certain processing of your personal information, and to request portability where applicable. You may also opt out of marketing emails at any time using the unsubscribe link or by contacting us.

10. Children's Privacy

Our Site is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

11. International Users

Our business is based in the United States. If you access the Site from outside the United States, your information may be processed in the United States in accordance with this Policy and applicable law.

12. Third-Party Links

The Site may include links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for their content or privacy practices. Please review their privacy policies before sharing personal information.

13. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any update becomes effective when posted on this page. Your continued use of the Site after updates are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised Policy.
