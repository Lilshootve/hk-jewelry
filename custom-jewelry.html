Custom Design Process

1

Consultation

Share your ideas or sketches with our team. We discuss design details, materials, and budget. (introduzca informacion aqui for any specific consultation info, e.g., appointment scheduling).

2

Design & Quote

Our designers will create a detailed design (including 3D renderings or sketches) for your approval. We also provide a quote for the project at this stage.

3

Craftsmanship

Upon approval, our master jewelers begin crafting your piece by hand, using the finest materials. Throughout this stage, we maintain communication and can share progress updates.

4

Completion & Delivery

Once finished, your bespoke jewelry piece is inspected for quality and presented to you. We can arrange secure shipping or in-person pickup at our Miami showroom.
